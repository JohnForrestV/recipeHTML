What are the Ingredients you need to make escondidinho?

    Filling

  • 1 tablespoon of oil
  • half a kilo of hard drumstick
  • 1 sliced onion
  • 2 chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tablet of MAGGI® Equilibrium Meat Broth
  • 2 tablespoons of chopped parsley

    • Potato

  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 300 g boiled and mashed potatoes
  • 300 g of boiled and mashed manioc
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 pinch of black pepper
  • half a cup (tea) of NINHO® Forti+ Whole Milk Liquid
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese

What are the steps to put together escondidinho?

  1. In a pressure cooker, heat the oil and brown the meat
  2. Add the onion, tomatoes, MAGGI Broth and 2 cups (tea) of water
  3. Cover the pan and let it cook for about 30 minutes after getting pressure.
  4. Turn off the heat, wait for the pressure to release, remove the pieces of meat and shred.
  5. Return the shredded meat to the pan and cook for another 10 minutes to thicken the sauce.
  6. Mix the parsley, transfer to a refractory container and set aside.
  7. Now for the potatoes! In a pan, melt the butter, add the potato and manioc and mix well.
  8. Add the salt, pepper and NINHO milk and stir until a homogeneous puree is formed
  9. Distribute the puree over the reserved meat, sprinkle the Parmesan cheese and take it to the medium-high oven (200°C), preheated, for about 15 minutes to brown.
